XBNB - The Ferrum Network

LaunchPad Technical services

Using the Ferrum Network XBNB users can stake in one token and receive staking rewards in another token. This ability is mirrored in XBNB’s SafeSwap exchange allowing listed projects to offer staking of their liquidity pool tokens for their own native tokens. As a part of the partnership, projects that list on XBNB’s Safe Swap will have fast-track access to Ferrum Network’s revolutionary Liquidity Staking technology. For instance, SafeSwap listed projects will be able to offer staking of their liquidity pool tokens — including the XBNB LP tokens — and offer rewards in their native tokens, thereby incentivizing and strengthening the XBNB liquidity pools. Ferrum and XBNB can offer cross-token staking regimes, where users can stake one project’s tokens and earn rewards in others native tokens.

Together, YFDAI and the Ferrum Network will allow for an exclusive XBNB experience as it merges the compatibilities of XBNBs DeFi options and SafeSwap exchange with Ferrum Network’s cross-chain capabilities. YFDAI and Ferrum are poised to unlock new possibilities in the XbNb space.
